How to Contest a Ticket

In-Person

Request an in-person hearing by calling the City of Chicago's Ticket Helpline @ 312.744.PARK (7275) or 312.744.7277 (TTY - Hearing Impaired).

Request an in-person hearing by mail. Fill the request for in-person hearing bubble on the ticket envelope or return the envelope provided with the mailed Notice of Violation. If responding from the mailed Notice of Violation, please fill the in-person hearing bubble both on the stub of the Notice of Violation and the envelope provided.

By Mail
